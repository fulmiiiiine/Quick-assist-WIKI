# Fun Commands

Here are the fun commands available in the Discord bot:

## /joke
Get a random joke.

## /flip
Flip a coin.

## /dance
Dance!

## /hug
Hug someone!

## /slap
Slap someone!

## /burn
Burn someone!

## /roll
Roll a random number between 1 and 100.

## /8ball
Ask the magic 8 ball a question.

## /choose
Let the bot choose between two options.
